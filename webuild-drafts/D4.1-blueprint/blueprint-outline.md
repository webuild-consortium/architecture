**Authors:**

## Overview

The **Architecture & Integration Blueprint Reference Document (D4.1)** is the foundational document that establishes the high-level framework for WE BUILDs technical execution.

To manage expectations and integrate D4.1 into the existing governance structure (ADR/CS process), the Blueprint should be treated as the strategic, overarching mandate that justifies the existence and direction of subsequent, specific technical specifications.

## Background

D4.1, due in Month 7, is tasked with developing the EUDI-compliant architecture blueprint.

Briefly describes the purpose of the Blueprint in achieving a scalable, compliant, and interoperable architectural framework for WE BUILD.
